Who We Are

GS Big J Dance Community

The GS Big J Dance Community is a Christ-centered dance ministry dedicated to using the art of dance to present Christ and His message to people, especially the younger generation. Our mission is to help young individuals enhance their dancing skills while guiding them toward a God-directed lifestyle. We aim to mold personal desires into Godly desires, train them to be effective leaders by drawing them closer to Christ, and build a community of believers, missionaries, preachers, and leaders who are impacted by and are a by-product of our ministry.

Our motto, "We dance to EXPRESS and not to IMPRESS," reflects our focus on genuine worship through dance, as inspired by Psalm 149:3, which says, "Let them praise His name in the dance..."
